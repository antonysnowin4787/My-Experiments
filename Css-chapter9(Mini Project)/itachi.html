ITACHI UCHIHA

Introduction

Itachi Uchiha is a legendary prodigy shinobi from Konohagakure's Uchiha clan, renowned for his immense intellect, speed, and mastery of the Mangekyō Sharingan. Forced to slaughter his clan to prevent a coup d'état, he acted as a double agent, joining the Akatsuki while secretly protecting Konoha and his younger brother, Sasuke.

  • Background: Born to Fugaku and Mikoto Uchiha, he was a child prodigy who witnessed the horrors of the Third Shinobi World War, making him a pacifist at heart.
  • Abilities: Possesses the Mangekyō Sharingan, allowing the use of Amaterasu (black flames), Tsukuyomi (genjutsu), and Susanoo with the Yata Mirror and Totsuka Blade
