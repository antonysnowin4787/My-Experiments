OBITO UCHIHA

Introduction

bito Uchiha is a central antagonist-turned-redeemer in Naruto, acting as the true leader of the Akatsuki, founder of the Fourth Shinobi World War, and former teammate to Kakashi Hatake. Believed dead during the Third Shinobi War, he was saved by Madara Uchiha, becoming disillusioned with the world after Rin Nohara's death, adopting the masks of "Tobi" and "Madara" to execute the "Eye of the Moon" plan.

  • Background: Born into the Konohagakure Uchiha clan, Obito was a kind-hearted, emotional ninja with a dream to become Hokage. He was part of Team Minato alongside Kakashi and Rin, mentored by Minato Namikaze.
  • Abilities & Power: Obito is incredibly powerful, wielding the Mangekyo Sharingan for the space-time technique Kamui (intangibility/teleportation). Following his rescue, his body was augmented with Hashirama Senju’s cells, granting him exceptional healing and Wood Style techniques. He later obtained the Rinnegan, becoming the Ten-Tails Jinchuriki during the Fourth Shinobi World War.
