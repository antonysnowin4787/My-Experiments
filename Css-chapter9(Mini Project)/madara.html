MADARA UCHIHA

Introduction

Madara Uchiha is a legendary leader of the Uchiha clan and co-founder of Konohagakure alongside his rival, Hashirama Senju. As a primary antagonist in Naruto: Shippuden, he sought to end humanity's conflict by forcing the world into an infinite dream (Infinite Tsukuyomi). He is renowned for his immense chakra, mastery of the Sharingan/Rinnegan, and role as the Ten-Tails' Jinchūriki.

  • Background: Born during the Warring States Period, he sought peace through strength and domination, eventually defecting from Konoha.
  • Abilities: Possesses the Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan and awakened the Rinnegan by infusing himself with Hashirama's cells.
