On Discipline

Elon works 80–100 hour weeks, not because he has to — but because the mission demands it. He schedules his day in 5-minute blocks, treating time as the most finite resource. Discipline, for him, is the bridge between ambition and reality.

On Consistency

SpaceX failed its first three rocket launches. Most people would have quit. Elon used the last of his money for a fourth attempt — and it succeeded. Consistency isn't about never failing; it's about refusing to stop.
